FIELD REPORTCase Study

Built for compact utility vehicles.

The XWD-48 replaces a heavier, slower-charging power system inside the same chassis footprint — engineered to withstand vibration, moisture and temperature swings across a full working season.

Heavy Machinery — Field Application
The pack had to fit the existing chassis, survive a full outdoor season, and charge fast enough to keep the fleet moving.

Engineering brief — compact utility & multi-tool carrier platform

01 — Challenge

Same footprint, more demand

The vehicle's compact chassis left no room to grow — the new pack had to match the original envelope while delivering more usable energy per shift.

02 — Approach

Sealed, modular, monitored

A modular 48V LFP pack with an IP67 enclosure and integrated i-BMS, designed for cell-level monitoring and thermal protection in an outdoor duty cycle.

03 — Result

Full-shift, all-season reliability

Consistent runtime across temperature swings, fast-charge turnaround between shifts, and a lighter overall vehicle for easier handling.
