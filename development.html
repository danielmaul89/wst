From Idea to Mass Production

We are a full-service custom battery pack manufacturer.

We design lithium battery packs according to your specifications, with design, engineering and manufacturing competences under one roof — this page walks through the customer journey, from first conversation to volume production.

Step 01 / 06

Use Case / Application

We start by understanding your application.

  • What are the performance requirements?
  • Which environment will the battery operate in?
  • What are your expectations for lifetime, safety, and integration?

Through a focused dialogue, we define the right foundation for your solution.

Step 02 / 06

Design Fase / Feasibility Study

We translate your requirements into a viable solution.

  • Technical feasibility, cost, and integration are evaluated early.
  • The result is a battery design optimized for performance, reliability, and production.
Step 03 / 06

Prototype and Testing

We develop and deliver a working prototype.

  • Together, we test it in your real-world application.
  • This ensures the solution performs as expected — before moving forward.
Step 04 / 06

Industrialization and Quality Management (EOL)

We prepare the design for scalable production.

  • Focus is on Design for Manufacturing (DFM) and robust processes.
  • Quality assurance and end-of-line (EOL) testing procedures are implemented from the start.
Step 05 / 06

Market Compliance

We ensure your solution meets all relevant standards.

  • From testing to documentation and approvals.
  • Your product is ready for global markets.
Step 06 / 06

Mass Production / SOP

Production is handled by our trusted partner, HAIDI, in China.

  • We ensure consistent quality, scalability, and full traceability.
  • Ready for delivery — at the right volume and cost.
