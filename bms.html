BMS Solutions

Battery management systems, engineered in-house.

Our BMS team designs the electronics and firmware around your pack — not the other way around. Four platforms cover the full range, from compact low-voltage packs to highly specialized, safety-critical systems.

Low Voltage

i-BMS

Our entry platform for compact, low-voltage packs — sized for robotics, drones and portable equipment where every gram and cubic centimetre counts.

  • Compact form factor for space-constrained packs
  • Cell balancing and protection tuned for small-format cells
  • CANBUS / UART communication as standard
High Voltage

n-BMS

A high-voltage platform built for heavy machinery, marine and industrial power systems that need to run all day, every day.

  • Scales across multi-module, high-voltage pack architectures
  • Robust protection for demanding duty cycles
  • Designed for integration with vehicle and machine control systems
Fast Charge

n3-BMS

Optimized for high-current fast-charge applications, where minimizing downtime between shifts or routes is the whole point.

  • Charge management engineered for minimal downtime
  • Thermal monitoring tuned for high-current charge cycles
  • Suited to material handling and last-mile delivery fleets
Highly Specialized

c-BMS24x

A configurable platform for specialized or highly regulated applications — defense, maritime and continuous-duty systems that need custom firmware.

  • Application-specific firmware development
  • Built for continuous, always-on monitoring
  • Custom protocol and integration work on request

Not sure which platform fits?

Tell us about your voltage class, duty cycle and charge requirements — we'll recommend the right BMS platform, or build a custom one.
