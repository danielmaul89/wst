DEEP-DIVETechnology

What's actually inside a WS Technicals pack.

Our in-house BMS platform handles cell-level monitoring, protection and balancing — designed around your pack's electrical architecture, not adapted from a generic module.

Cell chemistry and form factor are selected against your duty cycle and environment — energy density, cycle life and safety margin tuned to the application, not a catalogue default.

Thermal interface layers and enclosure design are engineered together, so the pack holds its performance envelope across the full operating temperature range.

Application-specific firmware and CANBUS integration are built around your control system — the pack speaks your vehicle's language from day one.
